Welcome to QISCET ReclaimHub

Helping students and staff reconnect with their lost belongings on campus.

Lost your ID, notes, or a personal item? Found something that doesn’t belong to you? ReclaimHub is here to make reporting, tracking, and returning items easy, safe, and fast.

I Lost Something I Found Something

How It Works

1

Report Your Item

Lost something or found an unclaimed item? Fill in the details and upload a photo so it’s easy to identify.

2

We Help You Find a Match

Our system checks for possible matches between lost and found items, making sure nothing slips through the cracks.

3

Get Notified & Reunite

When a match is found, you’ll get a notification so you can contact the owner or finder and get the item back safely.

About QISCET ReclaimHub

QISCET ReclaimHub helps students and staff report, track, and return lost items on campus. A safe and easy way to reconnect belongings with their owners.
